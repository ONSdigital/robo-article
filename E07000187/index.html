How the population has changed in Mendip

In 2020, Mendip was the 19th largest local authority in the South West, with a total population of 116,288. An increase compared with 2019.

Population density in Mendip

In 2020, the population density in Mendip was 157 people per square kilometre, which was less than the South West (237), and less than the United Kingdom as a whole (276).

Median age in Mendip

In 2020, the median age in Mendip was 47 years, which was older than the South West (44 years), and older than the United Kingdom as a whole (41 years).
