How the population has changed in Inverclyde

Demographic trends in Inverclyde and other local authorities within the UK from 2002 to 2020
Last updated:
01 January 2023

Inverclyde's demographics, 2020:

!
Warning:

This is a demo article. The numbers are based on official ONS mid-year population estimates, but may not be presented accurately.

Total population in Inverclyde

In 2020, Inverclyde was the 28th largest local authority in Scotland, with a total population of 77,060. A decrease compared with 2019.

Population density in Inverclyde

In 2020, the population density in Inverclyde was 480 people per square kilometre, which was more than Scotland (70), and more than the United Kingdom as a whole (276).

Median age in Inverclyde

In 2020, the median age in Inverclyde was 46 years, which was older than Scotland (42 years), and older than the United Kingdom as a whole (41 years).
