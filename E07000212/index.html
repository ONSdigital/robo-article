How the population has changed in Runnymede

Demographic trends in Runnymede and other local authorities within the UK from 2002 to 2020
Last updated:
01 January 2023

Runnymede's demographics, 2020:

!
Warning:

This is a demo article. The numbers are based on official ONS mid-year population estimates, but may not be presented accurately.

Total population in Runnymede

In 2020, Runnymede was the 58th largest local authority in the South East, with a total population of 90,327. No change compared with 2019.

Population density in Runnymede

In 2020, the population density in Runnymede was 1,157 people per square kilometre, which was more than the South East (483), and more than the United Kingdom as a whole (276).

Median age in Runnymede

In 2020, the median age in Runnymede was 37 years, which was younger than the South East (42 years), and younger than the United Kingdom as a whole (41 years).
