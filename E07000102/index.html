How the population has changed in Three Rivers

In 2020, Three Rivers was the 37th largest local authority in the East of England, with a total population of 93,966. An increase compared with 2019.

Population density in Three Rivers

In 2020, the population density in Three Rivers was 1,058 people per square kilometre, which was more than the East of England (328), and more than the United Kingdom as a whole (276).

Median age in Three Rivers

In 2020, the median age in Three Rivers was 42 years, which was the same as the East of England (42 years), and older than the United Kingdom as a whole (41 years).
