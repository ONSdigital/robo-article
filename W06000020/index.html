How the population has changed in Torfaen

Demographic trends in Torfaen and other local authorities within the UK from 2002 to 2020
Last updated:
01 January 2023

Torfaen's demographics, 2020:

!
Warning:

This is a demo article. The numbers are based on official ONS mid-year population estimates, but may not be presented accurately.

Total population in Torfaen

In 2020, Torfaen was the 18th largest local authority in Wales, with a total population of 94,832. An increase compared with 2019.

Population density in Torfaen

In 2020, the population density in Torfaen was 754 people per square kilometre, which was more than Wales (153), and more than the United Kingdom as a whole (276).

Median age in Torfaen

In 2020, the median age in Torfaen was 42 years, which was the same as Wales (42 years), and older than the United Kingdom as a whole (41 years).
