How the population has changed in Selby

In 2020, Selby was the 18th largest local authority in Yorkshire-Humber, with a total population of 91,697. An increase compared with 2019.

Population density in Selby

In 2020, the population density in Selby was 153 people per square kilometre, which was less than Yorkshire-Humber (359), and less than the United Kingdom as a whole (276).

Median age in Selby

In 2020, the median age in Selby was 45 years, which was older than Yorkshire-Humber (40 years), and older than the United Kingdom as a whole (41 years).
