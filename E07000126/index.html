How the population has changed in South Ribble

Demographic trends in South Ribble and other local authorities within the UK from 2002 to 2020
Last updated:
01 January 2023

South Ribble's demographics, 2020:

!
Warning:

This is a demo article. The numbers are based on official ONS mid-year population estimates, but may not be presented accurately.

Total population in South Ribble

In 2020, South Ribble was the 27th largest local authority in the North West, with a total population of 111,086. No change compared with 2019.

Population density in South Ribble

In 2020, the population density in South Ribble was 982 people per square kilometre, which was more than the North West (522), and more than the United Kingdom as a whole (276).

Median age in South Ribble

In 2020, the median age in South Ribble was 45 years, which was older than the North West (40 years), and older than the United Kingdom as a whole (41 years).
