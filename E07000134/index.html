How the population has changed in North West Leicestershire

In 2020, North West Leicestershire was the 22nd largest local authority in the East Midlands, with a total population of 104,809. An increase compared with 2019.

Population density in North West Leicestershire

In 2020, the population density in North West Leicestershire was 375 people per square kilometre, which was more than the East Midlands (311), and more than the United Kingdom as a whole (276).

Median age in North West Leicestershire

In 2020, the median age in North West Leicestershire was 44 years, which was older than the East Midlands (41 years), and older than the United Kingdom as a whole (41 years).
