How the population has changed in Tamworth

In 2020, Tamworth was the 29th largest local authority in the West Midlands, with a total population of 76,864. An increase compared with 2019.

Population density in Tamworth

In 2020, the population density in Tamworth was 2,491 people per square kilometre, which was more than the West Midlands (459), and more than the United Kingdom as a whole (276).

Median age in Tamworth

In 2020, the median age in Tamworth was 41 years, which was older than the West Midlands (40 years), and the same as the United Kingdom as a whole (41 years).
