How the population has changed in Basildon

In 2020, Basildon was the sixth largest local authority in the East of England, with a total population of 187,558. An increase compared with 2019.

Population density in Basildon

In 2020, the population density in Basildon was 1,705 people per square kilometre, which was more than the East of England (328), and more than the United Kingdom as a whole (276).

Median age in Basildon

In 2020, the median age in Basildon was 39 years, which was younger than the East of England (42 years), and younger than the United Kingdom as a whole (41 years).
