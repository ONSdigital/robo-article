How the population has changed in Greenwich

Demographic trends in Greenwich and other local authorities within the UK from 2002 to 2020
Last updated:
01 January 2023

Greenwich's demographics, 2020:

!
Warning:

This is a demo article. The numbers are based on official ONS mid-year population estimates, but may not be presented accurately.

Total population in Greenwich

In 2020, Greenwich was the 15th largest local authority in London, with a total population of 289,034. An increase compared with 2019.

Population density in Greenwich

In 2020, the population density in Greenwich was 6,109 people per square kilometre, which was more than London (5,727), and more than the United Kingdom as a whole (276).

Median age in Greenwich

In 2020, the median age in Greenwich was 35 years, which was younger than London (36 years), and younger than the United Kingdom as a whole (41 years).
